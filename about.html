CEO

Rohit Pal, hailing from Dadeldhura and currently residing in Mahendranagar, Nepal, is a multifaceted individual with a rich blend of experiences and interests. As the Farwest Project Lead at Code for Change, Rohit spearheads initiatives aimed at leveraging technology for social impact. His journey includes a stint as a Mathematics Teacher at both Superlink Coaching And Tuition Center and Morning Glory Higher Secondary School, where he imparts knowledge and nurtures young minds. Rohit's academic prowess is highlighted by his Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Information Technology from Tribhuvan University Institute of Science and Technology (IOST-BSc.CSIT), complemented by certifications such as the Professional Diploma In Computer Application (PDICA). A proactive member of his community, Rohit serves as the President of SNSC ECO CLUB, channeling his passion for environmental conservation and sustainability. In his pursuit of continuous learning, Rohit embarks on endeavors to master web development and Python programming, positioning himself as a dynamic force in the ever-evolving tech landscape. Through his endeavors, Rohit remains committed to making meaningful contributions to both his local community and the broader realm of technology and education.

Our Advisor

Asmita Ojha, a resident of Mahendranagar, Nepal, embodies the spirit of leadership and innovation in the tech industry. Currently serving as the Regional Ambassador of Province 7 at Girl's In Tech Nepal, she advocates for gender diversity and inclusion within the technology sector. As Secretary at Code For Change Farwest and a former College Representative, Asmita actively participates in initiatives aimed at utilizing technology for social impact and community development. Her academic journey includes studying at Tribhuvan University Institute of Science and Technology (IOST-BSc.CSIT) following her education at Kanchan Vidya Mandir Higher Secondary School/College. With a passion for learning, she embarks on her journey as a Python beginner and learner, eager to explore the vast possibilities within the realm of programming and technology. Through her diverse roles and engagements, Asmita remains committed to driving positive change and advancing both the tech industry and her community.
